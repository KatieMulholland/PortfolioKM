About Me

My name is Katie Mulholland and this is my about me page! I am 20 years old and I am a student at Coastal Carolina University. I am a junior and I study Digital Culture and Design with a minor in journalism. My interests include music, the outdoors, web design, and photography. I am inspired by photographers like Michael Kenna, Laura Zalenga, and Kyle Thompson. I am also inspired by things I see on Pinterest and Instagram. I also enjoy flat art and water photograpgy.

As a junior in college, I am still unsure of what I want to do with my career but there are a few ideas that I have. One is working for a news site like Buzzfeed. I want to be able to incoporate things I learn with web design and photography along with my knowledge in journalism. I would also like to do something with music one day, however I would like to incoporate that into another job. Finally, another aspiration I have would be to have my own blog or website to showcase people's creative work, whatever that may be.

I have a pretty broad but specific aesthetic. When it comes to photography I like water, self-portraits and simplicity. In terms of graphic and web design, I also like simplicity, escpecially in flat art. I am drawn to neutral and earth tones, along with black and white. I tend to lean towards a boho slash modern vibe when it comes to either decoration or designing something.
